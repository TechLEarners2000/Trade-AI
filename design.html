Product Requirements Document: AI Trading Assistant Platform Version: 1.0 Author: Jay Harish P Date: July 2026 Status: Draft 1. Overview 1.1 Purpose A web platform that lets retail investors track Indian stocks (NSE/BSE), chat with an AI assistant for market questions, and view historical + live + predicted price charts — combining a public stock data API with an LLM for conversational Q&A and insight generation. 1.2 Problem Statement Retail investors juggle multiple apps for stock quotes, news, and analysis. Existing tools either give raw numbers with no context, or "AI" tools that don't ground answers in real data. This product merges real market data with an LLM that can explain, summarize, and (with clear disclaimers) suggest — all in one dashboard. 1.3 Goals - Give users a single dashboard for real-time NSE/BSE stock data. - Let users ask natural-language questions about stocks/markets and get grounded answers. - Visualize historical price action and a clearly-labeled "predicted" trend line. - Keep the system legally safe (educational, not financial advice). 1.4 Non-Goals - Not a brokerage — no real order placement/execution. - Not a guaranteed-accuracy prediction engine. - No options/derivatives/F&O data in v1. 2. Target Users - Students and early-career developers/investors. - Retail investors wanting quick explanations of jargon alongside live data. - Hackathon/portfolio use case. 4. Key Features 4.1 Stock Dashboard - Search bar with autocomplete. - Stock detail page: price, change %, day range, 52-week range, volume, market cap, P/E, dividend yield, EPS, sector/industry. - Exchange toggle: NSE (.NS) / BSE (.BO). - Watchlist: multiple stocks refreshed on interval. - Trending/popular stocks list. 4.2 Live + Historical + Predicted Chart - Interactive candlestick/line chart. - Historical, Live, and Predicted (dashed/shaded projection) zones. - Overlays: moving averages (SMA/EMA), volume bars. 4.3 AI Chat Assistant - Chat panel (persistent side panel or full page). - Assistant flow: parse intent → call internal stock-data endpoint(s) → compose grounded natural-language answer. - Comparisons and educational explanations. 4.4 Market Overview Page - Snapshot of major sector performers. - Simple sector heatmap (gainers/losers). 5. Non-Functional Requirements - Rate-limit safety (caching). - Resilience (graceful degradation). - Disclaimer compliance: "Educational use only — not financial advice."
